## 8. Troubleshooting FAQ `[FAQPage schema]`

**My lights keep flickering — is that dangerous?**
It can be. Flickering is sometimes minor, but it can signal a loose connection or a panel issue that's worth catching early. I find the actual cause rather than guessing.

**Why does my breaker keep tripping?**
A breaker trips to protect the circuit — from an overload, a short, or a ground fault. Repeated tripping means something needs attention. I trace it to the source instead of just resetting it.

**Do you charge for a diagnostic visit?**
Yes, a flat diagnostic fee, and it applies toward the repair if you move forward. You get a quote on the fix before I do the work.

**How fast can you come out?**
For most non-emergency issues, usually within the same week. For anything urgent like a burning smell, call and I'll advise you right away.

**Do you also install outlets, switches, and GFCIs?**
Yes. New outlets and switches, GFCI and AFCI upgrades, USB outlets, and dedicated circuits for appliances and home offices — all code-compliant.

**What areas do you serve?**
San Luis Obispo, Atascadero, Paso Robles, and surrounding SLO County.
