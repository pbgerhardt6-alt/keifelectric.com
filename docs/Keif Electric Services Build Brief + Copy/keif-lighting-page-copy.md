## 3. Finish-quality work `[this is Adam's positioning differentiator — lighting is where it shows most]`

Lighting is where sloppy work shows. Cans that don't line up, trims that sit crooked, dimmers that buzz or flicker. I take the extra time to lay out spacing properly, mount level, and match trims so the finished room looks considered, not patched together.

If a fixture needs a dedicated circuit or the wiring behind it isn't up to code, I handle that too rather than working around it.

## 7. Lighting FAQ `[FAQPage schema]`

**Can you convert my old ceiling fixtures to recessed lighting?**
Yes. Retrofitting to recessed LED is one of the most common lighting jobs I do. I'll lay out spacing so the room lights evenly.

**Do I need a permit for lighting work?**
Simple fixture swaps usually don't. New circuits, exterior lighting, and larger projects often do. I'll tell you upfront and handle any permit needed.

**Can you install a chandelier on a high ceiling?**
Yes, with the correct fan/fixture-rated box and support. Heavy or high fixtures need the right mounting, which is exactly the part people shouldn't DIY.

**Do you install smart or dimmable lighting?**
Yes — dimmers, smart switches, and scene control, matched to fixtures that will actually dim without buzzing or flickering.

**What areas do you cover?**
San Luis Obispo, Atascadero, Paso Robles, and surrounding SLO County.
