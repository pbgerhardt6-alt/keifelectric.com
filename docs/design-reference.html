Frequently asked

Answers, before you pick up the phone.

Common questions about licensing, pricing, permits, and how we work. Don't see yours? Call (805) 286-0477.

Still have a question? Ask us
Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Keif Electric holds a California C-10 electrical contractor license and carries full liability and workers' compensation insurance. The license number is displayed on every quote and invoice, happy to email it to you before any visit.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, for most residential jobs. Simple repairs we can often quote by phone. For larger projects like panel upgrades or EV charger installs, we'll come out and assess at no cost, and you'll get a written estimate the same week.

What areas do you serve?

Atascadero, Paso Robles, Templeton, Santa Margarita, San Luis Obispo, Morro Bay, Cambria, Pismo Beach, Arroyo Grande, Los Osos, and Cayucos. If you're outside that footprint, just call , we travel for the right job.

How do you price jobs?

Flat-rate pricing whenever possible. You'll get a clear written quote before any work begins , no hidden fees, no surprise add-ons. For troubleshooting, we charge a diagnostic fee that's applied to the repair if you move forward.

Do you pull permits?

Yes, when required by code. Panel upgrades, EV charger installs, and new circuits typically require permits, we handle the paperwork and the inspections, so you don't have to.

How soon can you come out?

For non-emergency work we typically schedule within a few business days. We're available Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm. Call early in the week for the best chance at a same-week slot.

Do you install EV chargers?

Yes, Level 2 chargers for Tesla, Rivian, Ford, and all electric vehicles. We pull the permit, handle inspection, and confirm your panel can support the load before we start running conduit.
